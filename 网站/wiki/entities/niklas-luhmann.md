# Niklas Luhmann

## Overview
German sociologist (1927-1998) who developed the [[zettelkasten]] (slip box) method of note-taking and knowledge management. His system of interconnected index cards enabled him to produce over 70 books and hundreds of articles.

## Key Contributions
- **Zettelkasten method**: Systematic approach to knowledge management
- **Sociological theory**: Systems theory applied to social structures
- **Productivity**: Demonstrated the power of networked note-taking
