# Vannevar Bush

## Overview
American engineer and science administrator (1890-1974) who conceptualized the [[memex]] in his 1945 article "As We May Think." His vision of a mechanical device for storing and retrieving knowledge laid the groundwork for hypertext and modern knowledge management systems.

## Key Contributions
- **Memex concept** (1945): Predicted personal knowledge bases with associative linking
- **Science administration**: Led U.S. scientific research during WWII
- **Influence**: Inspired Douglas Engelbart, Ted Nelson, and later hypertext pioneers
